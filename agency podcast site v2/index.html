Agency

Some agency for your digital agency.

My name is Alison Ostendorf.

I am a freelance UX designer and startup consultant, and I am interviewing digital design and development agencies in the US.

My goal: Get a clear picture of how widely the web design and services industry varies, company by company, and why.

Then write a book about it.

Specifically, I am asking each design agency questions like:

  • Do you believe that you company is the best in your area?
  • How much do you charge?
  • How much of that do you get to keep?
  • How many projects do you work on per month?
  • Who actually does the work?
  • Do you use offshore talent?
  • How do you find clients?
  • What advice can you offer other design agencies? Prospective clients?
  • What is most missing in you business right now?

Do you run a web design / development agency?
Would you like to be involved?
(Interviews can be anonymous upon request.)
